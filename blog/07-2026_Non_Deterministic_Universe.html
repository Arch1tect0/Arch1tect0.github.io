Is our Universe "Deterministic", "non-deterministic" or neither?

When we talk about how the universe operates and functions, we need to think about ideas like agency and free will. It's more of philosophical question, rather than a scientific one.

That said, it seems to follow very simple rules, but very hard for us to think about the very fabric of the system,since we live in it. Questions arise, such as "Do we have free will?".

On that note I've wrestling for a while with the idea of a computational system or algorithms as you may say; Is our universe probablistic? deterministic? or neither?

My thought process and research focus on answering that very question, but to give you an initial idea, I don't think it's either of those. It seems to operate on somewhat of a non-deterministic vs. deterministic state. These are just speculations for now and I don't have anything concrete yet. Later I'll try to come up with a better explanation! (hopefully!)
