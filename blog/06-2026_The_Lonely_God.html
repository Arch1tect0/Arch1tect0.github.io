The lonley God...

So I have been reading the Old testament, the new one and the Quran. One can argue that it's not necessary to read the religious books anymore, but I'd say it's very important, first of all because while the modern life has made a lot of things easier and more accessible, we have a foundational need for purpose. That purpose and foundation if be it the Truth or God, I argure that's the best thing. Second, these books have affected us culturally during the generations so much that it's undeniable even if you're against the actual content. For me, while trying to be open-minded I was very cautious to examine the materials (as much as one could be!). here are my thoughts:

The ancient god, The Torah or the old testament is a very long, and difficult read espesially to a modern reader without prior knowledge. it is more like a geneology written by ancient people. But I'd argue that it has value even today. My favorite parts...
