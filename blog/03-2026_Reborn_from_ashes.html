Reborn from Ashes

It all started with Linux. Well, not really! But Linux was what pushed me off the cliff. So I was technically already there — I just needed a little nudge.

If none of this makes any sense to you, it's because it's my first post ever, and because I’m always inside that space in my head. I need to communicate a few things before any of this starts making sense.

I have been wanting to write for a while. I used to do it before. I always wanted to become a writer, and I had some projects going on back in the day, but I dropped that to pursue other things — science, for example. Little did I know...

Now, after a long time, I have the need to talk again, to share, to write. I don’t want to label it as if I have something cool or valuable to say. It’s that simple. I just want to share. I want to be seen.

Anyway, recently, due to some life circumstances, I was pushed again to reclaim my peace — to start fighting again — because life in general seemed so chaotic and out of reach. I was doing things, but none of them were very meaningful. It started with a period of anxiety, then depression, and here I am: a clearer mind that can manage things. Very basic, but at least I can tell wrong from wrong again. I can feel and possibly enjoy the taste of a meal I prepare. I enjoy the fresh air and the beautiful sunset outside. I get to feel all those various feelings that life has to offer, and smell all the good and bad smells, just like the taste when I’m outside.

All of that might seem simple, yet they’re vital things that I lose touch with if I don’t dedicate time to them — if I get pulled back into the regular noise of modern life.

Anyway, long story short, I went back and read some books and, more importantly, did some thinking. I tried to acquire a lifestyle that is less materialistic. Yes — at the end of the day, I believe that was the source of all my problems and will be again. So I will have to strengthen my soul to fight that and get back to the core frequency I can operate on, while maintaining a balance of thinking and health.

And that’s where I came upon Linux — a free, lightweight, open‑source piece of software that almost all the devices you and I are currently interacting on are somewhat based on.

It’s very simple but very, very, very effective.

So hello there, fellow thinker! If you’re here reading this, it means your soul longs for some deeper truth just like mine. It’s a pleasure to meet you. I may not know what you look like or what your name is, but deep down we have a strong connection.

Stay with me. I’d do it alone, but I don’t like to journey alone. I want to be seen. I want to do it with you.
